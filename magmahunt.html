MagmaPlay's Custom Manhunt Plugin

Posted: May 13, 2024
Built for a Minecraft Paper server (version 1.20.4), this plugin introduces a custom twist on Manhunt gameplay.

Use the command /setrunner <player> to assign a player as the "runner." That player will:
  • Receive extremely overpowered loot when breaking oak logs
  • Gain strong potion effects when taking damage
This adds a chaotic and strategic element to the classic Manhunt mode, making the chase more intense and unpredictable.
